A life built here

More than a market. A place I call home.

I know what it feels like to arrive on the Seacoast and realize it is more than a beautiful place to live. I came here after earlier chapters in Traverse City, Vail, and New York City, and more than 25 years later this is where I raised my family, built relationships, and learned the rhythm of each community.

That perspective shapes how I work. I do not want clients to feel rushed, talked over, or pushed into a decision that only looks right on paper. I want them to understand the home, the neighborhood, the tradeoffs, and the next step.
