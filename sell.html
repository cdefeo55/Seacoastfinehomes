Sell with a clear plan.

Selling a home is financial, practical, and often emotional. I help you prepare the property, understand the market, and move through the process with a plan instead of guesswork.

How I approach selling.

I believe a good sale starts before the home goes live. We talk about what should be repaired, what should be simplified, how the home should be presented, and what buyers in your price range are likely to notice.

Pricing is not about chasing a number from an algorithm. It is about reading the current market, comparable homes, buyer behavior, condition, timing, and the story your home can tell when it is presented well.

Throughout the process, I keep communication steady. You should know what is happening, why it matters, and what I recommend next.

Seller support includes:

Pricing strategy, preparation advice, staging guidance, photography coordination, launch timing, showing feedback, negotiation, inspection response, and closing support.
