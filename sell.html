Seller strategy

Preparation and positioning matter more than ever.

Seacoast buyers are often comparing homes across several nearby towns. That means your listing needs to make the home’s value clear quickly: location, condition, layout, setting, lifestyle, and what makes it hard to replace.

Chantal helps sellers make smart pre-listing decisions, avoid unnecessary spending, and create a launch plan that gives the home its best chance to stand out.

Seller support includes

  • Local pricing guidance and comparable sale review.
  • Pre-listing preparation and staging recommendations.
  • Property story, photography direction, and launch copy.
  • Negotiation strategy and steady transaction management.
