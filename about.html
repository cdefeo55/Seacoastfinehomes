Meet Chantal

A Seacoast local with a relationship-first approach to real estate.

Chantal O'Hare has lived on the Seacoast of New Hampshire for more than 25 years. She brings local perspective, client-service experience, and a calm, personal style to every buyer and seller relationship.

Her story

Helping people find homes that become part of their lives.

Chantal’s Seacoast roots are central to how she works. After more than two decades living in the area, she understands the small details that make each town feel different: the beaches, neighborhoods, back roads, school-day routines, commuting patterns, local restaurants, and the quiet pockets people only learn by being here.

Her goal is not to push people into a decision. It is to help them understand the market, evaluate the tradeoffs, and feel confident about the next step.

  • More than 25 years living on the Seacoast of New Hampshire.
  • Licensed Sales Associate in New Hampshire and Maine.
  • Based with Tate & Foss Sotheby’s International Realty in Rye.
  • Known for communication, patience, negotiation, and follow-through.
What clients need

Real estate advice that feels human.

For buyers

Chantal helps buyers compare towns, understand market dynamics, and stay grounded when the process gets competitive or emotional.

For sellers

She helps sellers prepare the home, position the story, price with discipline, and manage the details from listing through closing.

For relocations

She helps out-of-area buyers understand the Seacoast like a local, narrowing the search around lifestyle instead of just listings.
