Her story

Local perspective, calm guidance, and a deep love for the place she calls home.

I know what it feels like to arrive on the Seacoast and realize it is more than a beautiful place to live.

I came here after chapters in Traverse City, Vail, and New York City, and like so many people who find their way to this corner of New Hampshire, I fell in love with the pace, the coastline, the neighborhoods, the seasons, and the sense of community. What started as a move became a life. More than 25 years later, the Seacoast is where I raised my family, built lasting relationships, and came to understand this area not just as a market, but as home.

That lived experience is at the heart of how I work with clients.

When someone is buying here, I know they are not just choosing square footage or a town on a map. They are imagining morning walks near the water, school drop-offs, weekend games, favorite restaurants, quiet streets, beach days, holiday traditions, and the everyday rhythm of a life that feels right. When someone is selling, I understand they may be letting go of a home filled with years of memories, milestones, and meaning.

That perspective matters.

Buying or selling a home here is not just about comparing properties. It is about understanding lifestyle, timing, value, community, and what will truly feel right once the papers are signed and life begins in that home.

Before real estate, I built a career rooted in service, communication, and trust. My years in hospitality taught me how important it is to listen closely, follow through, anticipate needs, and make people feel cared for throughout the process. My background in the investment industry, along with earning my Series 7 and 63 licenses, gave me a strong foundation in financial decision-making, preparation, negotiation, and protecting a client’s best interests.

Today, I bring all of that together for my clients: local insight, financial perspective, steady communication, and a deep love for the place I am fortunate to call home.

Whether you are moving to the Seacoast for the first time, searching for the right neighborhood, selling a longtime family home, or looking for a property that fits the next chapter of your life, my goal is simple: to help you make a confident decision, protect your best interests, and guide you through the process with honesty, discretion, and a calm hand from start to finish.
