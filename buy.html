Buy with local perspective.

Buying here is not just about finding a house. It is about understanding how each town, neighborhood, commute, beach route, and property style will actually feel once you live there.

How I help buyers think clearly.

I start by listening carefully. Some buyers know they want Portsmouth or Rye. Others know they want a certain feeling: near the water, close to restaurants, more privacy, a better commute, or a home that can grow with them.

From there, I help you compare options in a grounded way. We look at condition, location, resale considerations, renovation needs, flood exposure when relevant, and how aggressive the strategy needs to be if the right home appears.

My goal is not to talk you into a house. My goal is to help you recognize the right one and feel prepared when it is time to act.

Buyer conversations often include:

Town fit, neighborhood rhythm, property condition, offer strategy, inspections, financing timelines, coastal considerations, and resale value.
