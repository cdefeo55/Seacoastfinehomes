Seacoast Communities

Compare Seacoast NH towns before you choose where to buy or sell.

Each Seacoast town has its own feel, housing patterns, buyer demand, and lifestyle tradeoffs. Start here, then talk with Chantal about what fits your goals.

Portsmouth

Portsmouth is one of the Seacoast’s most recognizable markets, known for its walkable downtown, historic architecture, waterfront setting, restaurants, shops, and year-round energy.

Explore Portsmouth real estate

Rye

Rye offers a quieter coastal lifestyle with beaches, conservation land, established neighborhoods, and quick access to Portsmouth.

Explore Rye real estate

Hampton

Hampton brings together beach energy, year-round neighborhoods, commuter access, condos, single-family homes, and investment-minded opportunities.

Explore Hampton real estate

Exeter

Exeter has a classic New England feel with a historic downtown, riverfront character, local shops, and neighborhoods that appeal to a wide range of buyers.

Explore Exeter real estate

Dover

Dover offers a lively downtown, mill-building character, established neighborhoods, and a broader range of housing options than many smaller Seacoast towns.

Explore Dover real estate

Durham

Durham is closely tied to the University of New Hampshire and offers a mix of village convenience, neighborhood living, and access to the Oyster River area.

Explore Durham real estate

Stratham

Stratham is known for its residential feel, open spaces, farm-country character, and convenient access to Exeter, Portsmouth, and commuter routes.

Explore Stratham real estate

North Hampton

North Hampton offers a coastal-adjacent residential feel with convenient access to Portsmouth, Rye, Hampton, and Route 1 services.

Explore North Hampton real estate

Greenland

Greenland offers a small-town residential setting with close access to Portsmouth, Rye, commuter routes, and Seacoast amenities.

Explore Greenland real estate

New Castle

New Castle is one of the Seacoast’s most distinctive communities, with island character, historic homes, water views, and limited inventory.

Explore New Castle real estate

Hampton Falls

Hampton Falls has a rural-residential feel, with open land, established homes, and access to nearby beaches, commuter routes, and town centers.

Explore Hampton Falls real estate

Seabrook

Seabrook sits at the southern edge of the New Hampshire Seacoast and offers beach access, commuter convenience, and a range of residential options.

Explore Seabrook real estate
